### Text to Speech (TTS)

**[[#espeak-ng]]**
**[[#festival]]**

---
#### espeak-ng

https://github.com/espeak-ng/espeak-ng

**Supported platforms**
- Linux, BSD
- MacOS
- Windows

**Examples**

	espeak-ng "Hello world!"

---
#### festival

https://github.com/festvox/festival

**Supported platforms**
- Linux, BSD
- MacOS
- Windows

**Examples**

Speak text that has been selected with the cursor:

	xsel | festival --tts
